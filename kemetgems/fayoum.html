FAYOUM

Fayoum is one the Biggest Oasis in the World and the closest to the Nile River. One of the most preferable places not only for foreigners but also for Egyptians for its natural beauty, magnificent weather & its monuments that date back to several ages throughout history.

Fayoum word comes from the Coptic word 'Phiom', which means lake or sea since it was enclosing a sea in the Prehistoric period.

Fayoum remained a savage site for hunting and extracting mining salts. Thus, the Ancient Egyptians identified the lake of Fayoumwith some appropriate names, such as 'the Nun' or the Primeval Ocean, where life began.

Fayoum is a perfect sport for stars gazing.

Some of Fayoum sightseeing & activities:

  • Qaroun Palace & Lake
  • Valley Of Whales (Wadi ElHitan)
  • Wadi ElRayan Water Fall
  • Fayoum Water Wheel
  • Hawara Pyramid
  • Senferu Pyramid
  • Lahoun Pyramid
  • Temple of Dionysias (Temple of Stones)
  • Karanis Museum
  • Kom Oshim Museum
  • Caricature Museum
  • Sand Boarding & Safari
