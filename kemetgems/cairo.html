CAIRO & GIZA

Cairo is capital of Egypt. One of the largest cities in Africa. Cairo has stood for more than 1,000 years on the same site on the banks of the Nile River.

Cairo has acted for an infinite time as a guardian and a center for culture, art & beauty. It is quite famous for being the largest metropolitan area in Africa, The Middle East, the Arab world and the 15th largest in the world.

Cairo means in Arabic language: “The Victorious”.of the ancient world, and the legendary Library of Alexandria and was once the most vital cultural center of the ancient world.

Giza is most famous for containing the famous Giza Pyramids Complex that holds one of the Seven Wonders of the World the Great Pyramid of King Khufu, the world’s oldest and biggest statue the Sphinx and the house of mummification the Valley temple.
